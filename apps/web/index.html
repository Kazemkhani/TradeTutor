Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit the form?
Our AI builds a personalized call script using the information you provide, then places a phone call to each lead within seconds. The AI introduces itself, asks qualification questions, and handles the conversation based on your chosen goal.
Does the AI identify itself on the call?
Yes. The AI always identifies itself as an AI assistant and notifies the recipient that the call may be recorded. This is both a legal requirement and our commitment to transparency.
What phone number format should I use?
Use international format with a country code, like +1 555 123 4567 for US numbers or +44 20 7123 4567 for UK numbers. The plus sign and country code are required.
How many leads can I call at once?
You can add up to 5 leads per submission. Each lead gets a personalized call with their own name, company, and contact details factored into the conversation.
Is my data stored permanently?
No. Call records are ephemeral and automatically expire after 10 minutes. We don't maintain a database of your leads or call history. Form data saved in your browser expires after 30 minutes.
What AI technologies power the calls?
Calls use LiveKit for real-time voice infrastructure, Deepgram Nova-3 for speech recognition, ElevenLabs for natural-sounding text-to-speech, and GPT-4o-mini for conversational intelligence. Context is pre-built using Stanford DSPy.
